Who We Are

Master Craftsmen Dedicated to Perfection

FAROOQ & SONS has been a trusted name in premium carpentry and woodworking for over two decades. Founded by Haji Farooq with a vision to deliver unmatched quality, our family-run business has grown into one of the most respected woodworking studios in the region.

Every piece we create tells a story — of carefully selected timber, meticulous hand-finishing, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' visions. From elegant living room furniture to complete kitchen transformations, we bring warmth and character to every space.
